Social features for every app

Tapglue offers fully customizable plug & play tools to turn your product into a social network.

NEWS FEEDS

Build social news feeds that drive retention, engagement and personalization.

FULLY CUSTOMIZABLE

Create a lively experience around your existing content and your user's actvity.

LIKES, COMMENTS, SHARES

Built-in likes, comments and shares will make sure your own and your user's content is spread.

USER GENERATED CONTENT

Display user posts, events, images and more to create new ways to engage your users.

USER PROFILES

Create a community by adding user profiles to your product.

PROFILE PICTURE

Let users add and change pictures or sync with Facebook.

USER INFORMATION

Add any kind of user profile information and preferences.

FOLLOWER & FRIENDS

Display the number of followers or friends.

USER ACTIVITY

Display user-based activity feeds and timelines.

BOOMARKS & MORE

Let users create bookmarks, wishlists, favorites, watchlists and much more.

NOTIFICATIONS

Keep users posted about what's happening in their network.

FLEXIBLE ENTRIES

Define the events and notifications you want to display - no matter if it's a like, changing a profile picture or getting a new follower.

NOTIFICATION BADGES

Display unread badges in-app or on a user's home screen to leverage your community's activity and drive retention in a highly relevant way.

FRIENDS AND FOLLOWERS

Create open or private networks to create a powerful social graph around your product.

PRIVATE OR PUBLIC

Choose between the friends or the follower model for your network.

CONNECT FACEBOOK, TWITTER & CO.

Leverage Facebook, Twitter or the address book for "Find Friends".

USER SEARCH

Let users search for others to find people they can connect with.
