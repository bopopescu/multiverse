About Us

Tapglue’s aim is to skyrocket your app’s engagement and retention rate. After fifteen years of product, growth, and engineering experience in fast-growing tech companies, we’ve learned that the best way to increase retention and virality is to take advantage of network effects.

Our mission is simple: Tapglue enables you to to add a social layer to your app in under two hours allowing you to focus on creating an awesome user experience and growing your community.

With Tapglue’s social layer, you can harness the power of connected networks, letting users create personal profiles, connect with their friends, and foster maximum engagement.
